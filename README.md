**ADHD-FOCUS** is a tool designed specifically for neurodivergent individuals (ADHD) who struggle with time perception ("time blindness") and maintaining attention.

We stripped away everything unnecessary. No complex settings, no intrusive ads, and no paid subscriptions. Just pure focus.

* Instead of abstract digital numbers, the timer uses a red sector that physically shrinks over time. This allows your brain to instantly "see" how much time is left, reducing anxiety and urgency paralysis.
